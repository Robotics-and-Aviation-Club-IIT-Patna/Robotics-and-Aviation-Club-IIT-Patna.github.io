Welcome To RnA

The Robotics and Drone Aviation Club (RnA Club) of IIT Patna is a student - running organization dedicated to promoting the field of robotics and drone aviation. We aim to provide a platform for students to learn about, develop, and apply Robotics and Drone technology. We also organize various events and workshops to foster innovation and collaboration in this field. In the past few years, our members have won several awards at national and international competitions.

"Where robots take flight and dreams take off."
